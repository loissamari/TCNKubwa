üìã Executive Summary

Current State

  • Church: The Covenant Nation (TCN) Kubwa Branch
  • Resident Pastor: Pst Dr Eze
  • Target Audience: Young professionals, singles, young families (21-45 years)
  • Instagram Followers: Less than 200
  • Engagement: Currently poor

Core Positioning

"Community-oriented growth in the Word of God"

TCN Kubwa is positioned as a warm, inviting space for all ages with a particular strength in serving the 21-45 demographic. The church provides solid biblical teaching, career and family building support, and a genuine community atmosphere.

Primary Challenges

  • Low brand awareness in Kubwa and environs
  • Minimal social media engagement
  • Need to attract new members while retaining current ones
  • Competition from other churches (though most aren't strong online)

Strategic Approach

Dual-brand architecture with integrated Instagram + WhatsApp ecosystem

We'll run two interconnected brands:

  • @tcnkubwa - The church community brand ("we")
  • @pstdreze - Pastor's personal teaching brand ("I")

Both will be supported by a robust WhatsApp strategy including broadcast channels and segmented community groups.

Key Differentiators

  • Focus on practical life application (career + family)
  • Strong youth and young family engagement
  • Clear, warm, inspiring teaching style from Pst Dr Eze
  • Community-first approach vs. transaction-focused ministry

üèóÔ∏è Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-2)

A. Dual Brand Architecture

Brand 1: @tcnkubwa (Church Brand)

  • Purpose: The community, the experience, the "we"
  • Positioning: "Your Community. Your Growth. Your Home." OR "Building Lives in the Heart of Kubwa"
  • Visual Identity: Maintain TCN's core colors but add signature accent color (vibrant orange or teal)
  • Platforms: Instagram (primary) + WhatsApp Broadcast & Groups

Brand 2: @pstdreze (Pastor's Personal Brand)

  • Purpose: His voice, his teaching, the "I"
  • Positioning: "The Teachable Teacher" / "Your Guide to Purposeful Growth"
  • Visual Style: Personal, behind-the-scenes, conversational with signature teaching setting
  • Platforms: Instagram (3-4x/week) + WhatsApp Broadcast (daily devotionals)

B. Why Both Brands?

  • Church account builds community and belonging ("Come join us")
  • Pastor's account builds authority and relationship ("Learn from me")
  • They cross-pollinate: Pastor drives people to church, church elevates pastor's visibility
  • Reaches different audience segments with appropriate messaging

C. Brand Identity Quick Wins

For TCN Kubwa:

  • Create memorable tagline emphasizing community + growth
  • Establish visual distinction within TCN guidelines
  • Define 4 clear content pillars aligned with audience needs
  • Design consistent templates for all content types

For Pst Dr Eze:

  • Leverage his clear, warm, inspiring communication style
  • Highlight education and biblical depth
  • Showcase people-friendly, accessible teaching approach
  • Create signature visual element for teaching content

üìù Phase 2: Content Strategy (90 Days)

TCN KUBWA Content Pillars

1. COMMUNITY (30% of content)

Goal: Make people feel like they're missing out on belonging

  • Service highlights with story-focused narrative (not just photos)
  • Member spotlights: "Meet the TCN Kubwa Family"
  • Behind-the-scenes: prep, choir practice, ushering teams
  • Connection moments and fellowship highlights
  • Visitor welcome stories and testimonies

2. GROWTH (40% of content)

Goal: Position TCN Kubwa as where you level up spiritually

  • Sermon clips (30-60 seconds of pure gold from Pst Eze)
  • Weekly devotional graphics with key insights
  • "Monday Motivation" series from Sunday's message
  • Testimony Tuesdays (written or video format)
  • Scripture deep-dives and biblical teaching
  • Application-focused teaching moments

3. PRACTICAL LIFE (20% of content)

Goal: Show you care about their WHOLE life, not just Sundays

  • Career tips through a biblical lens
  • Marriage and relationship wisdom
  • Parenting insights for young families
  • Financial stewardship and biblical wealth principles
  • Life application of scripture to daily challenges
  • Book and resource recommendations

4. INVITATION (10% of content)

Goal: Clear calls to action for engagement

  • Service reminders with anticipation building
  • Event announcements and registration
  • New visitor information and welcome
  • Connection opportunities (small groups, serving)
  • Next steps for spiritual growth

PST DR EZE Content Pillars

1. TEACHING MOMENTS (50%)

  • 60-90 second teaching clips with clear takeaways
  • "Question of the Week" addressing DM questions
  • Scripture breakdowns and exposition
  • Book recommendations with why they matter
  • Deep theological insights made accessible

2. PERSONAL JOURNEY (30%)

  • Study routine and sermon preparation process
  • What he's currently learning and reading
  • Ministry moments and pastoral reflections
  • Family-appropriate personal stories
  • Behind-the-scenes of message development

3. ENGAGEMENT (20%)

  • Polls and questions for audience interaction
  • "Ask Pastor" stories and posts
  • Live Q&A sessions (monthly recommended)
  • Interactive theological discussions
  • Community building moments

Content Balance Strategy

The 90-day content strategy focuses on establishing consistency while testing what resonates with your audience. Each pillar serves a specific purpose in the customer journey:

  • Community content ‚Üí Creates belonging and FOMO
  • Growth content ‚Üí Establishes authority and value
  • Practical life content ‚Üí Shows holistic care
  • Invitation content ‚Üí Drives action and attendance
